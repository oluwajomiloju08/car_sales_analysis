## Car Sales Performance Analysis
## Overview
This project explores vehicle sales data to evaluate sales performance, revenue trends, and customer purchasing behaviour using Excel and Power BI.
## Business Problem
The objective of this project was to identify high-performing vehucle brands, monitor sales growth, and uncover opportunities to improve business performance.
## Tools Used
- Excel.
- Power BI.
## Data Preparation
Used Microsoft Excel to merge and clear two separate car sales datasets, creating a structured dataset suitable for analysis, reporting, and dashboard visualization.
## Skills Demonstration
- Data Cleaning.
- KPI Reporting.
- Trend analysis.
- Data Viaualization.
## Dashboard Features 
- Sales Growth KPIs.
-  Revenue Tracking.
- Monthly Sales Trends.
- Vehicle Brand Performance.
## Key Insights
- Lexus generated the highest total revenue among all the vehicle brands, making it the strongest contributor to overall sales performance.
- Jaguar Recorded the lowest sales volume and revenue, indicating weaker market demand compared to the other brands.
- Sales performance peaked in the 11th and 12th month, revealing seasonal purchasing trends that influenced overall revenue.
- Red-colored vehicles experienced a sales slump in YTD Sales across multiple brands, indicating lower customer demand for that color category.
## Recommendations
- Increase marketing efforts for top-performing vehicle model such as Lexus and Oldsmobile to maximize revenue generation.
- Monitor seasonal sales trends to improve inventory and sales planning.
- Introduce targeted promotional strategies for underperforming vehicle categories.
- Offer promotions or discounts to low purchasimg brands to drive purchases.
